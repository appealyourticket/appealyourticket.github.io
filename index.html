How To Contest

If you think one of these apply to you, there is a big chance you might win....

  • Invisible Parking Sign
  • Cited while valid permit was displayed
  • Car was stolen or you were not the vehicle owner when ticket was issued
  • You were issued an incorrect ticket or overcharged
  • The parking meter malfunctioned
  • Parking enforcement officers failed to present name properly

Next we will tell you how to appeal easily.

Preparations

Contest Ticket Template (Google Docs)
Contest Ticket Template (Download .docx)

First, you need the document above. Click on "Contest Ticket Template" and make yourself a copy. You will need to fill out this document before appealing to the court. Follow the guide present in the file. Key points:

  • Add name, address, zip code, phone number and email.
  • Put in current date, and adress the judge respectfully.
  • Fill in name, ticket date & location, license plate number, and ticket number.
  • Next, state the offense, and choose the defense that fits your situation. Be careful, deviating away from the ones listed might result in losing your appeal.
  • Prepare documents/photos as evidence for your defense.

When you are done preparing the documents, send them through email (preferred) or present them in person at a hearing (not likely). Key points at a hearing:

  • Be respectful, address them as your honor, sir, or ma'am.
  • Keep to your defense, state only true facts.
  • Do not attack officers' credibility, remain humble.
  • Show the court that the offense would not happen again.

With everything prepared, go to the hearing and win your appeal!
