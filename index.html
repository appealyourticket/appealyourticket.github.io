Who are we?

As a local nonprofit organization, our mission is to help people contest and win appeals on parking violations in the city of Chicago. We’re currently focusing on the 24th ward due to some alarming statistics that can be found here and down below.

Statistics

24th ward has 48.9 million dollars total in debt. Only 47% of the tickets are paid

46% tickets issued to motorists are at risk of losing vehicle or license

Only 9% of tickets are contested, but 67% of the contested tickets are not found liable

How To Appeal

If you think one of these apply to you, there is a big chance you might win....

  • Invisible Parking Sign
  • Cited while valid permit was displayed
  • Car was stolen or you were not the vehicle owner when ticket was issued
  • You were issued an incorrect ticket or overcharged
  • The parking meter malfunctioned
  • Parking enforcement officers failed to present name properly
